# 🛒 E-Commerce Customer Intelligence & Order Analytics System

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing e-commerce data to extract meaningful insights related to customer behavior, sales performance, and product trends. The goal is to support data-driven decision-making for business growth and operational efficiency.

The project was developed as part of a group internship, with a focus on data analysis, KPI generation, and business insights.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze customer purchase behavior and segmentation
* Track revenue and sales trends over time
* Identify top-performing customers and products
* Generate actionable insights for business decision-making

---

## 🧠 Business Analysis Approach

* Defined key business requirements and KPIs such as revenue, order volume, and customer retention
* Identified business problems like lack of visibility into sales performance and customer behavior
* Translated raw data into actionable insights for stakeholders

---

## 🔄 SDLC & Methodology

* Followed a **Waterfall approach** during the initial phase for requirement gathering, data understanding, and planning
* Implemented an **Agile approach** during execution by dividing the project into phases (data preparation, analysis, visualization)
* Simulated project tracking using Jira concepts such as Epics, User Stories, and Sprint planning

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

* Python (Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn)
* SQL (Joins, Aggregations, KPI queries)
* Jupyter Notebook
* Power BI / Tableau (for visualization)
* Jira (conceptual use for Agile workflow)

---

## 📊 Dataset

Used the Brazilian E-Commerce Public Dataset (Olist), which includes:

* Customers data
* Orders data
* Order items
* Payments data
* Products data

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* A small percentage of customers contribute significantly to total revenue
* Certain products dominate overall sales performance
* Monthly revenue trends highlight business growth patterns
* Repeat customers play a critical role in long-term profitability
